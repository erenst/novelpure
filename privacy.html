Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

1. Introduction / 简介

NovelPure is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the extension.

NovelPure 致力于保护您的隐私。本政策说明当您使用本扩展程序时，信息将如何被处理。

2. Information Handling / 信息处理方式

No Personal Data Collection / 不收集个人数据

NovelPure does not collect or transmit personally identifiable information.

NovelPure 不收集或传输任何可识别个人身份的信息。

Local Processing Only / 仅本地处理

All processing occurs locally within your browser. Reading preferences, theme settings, and reading progress are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.

所有处理均在您的浏览器本地进行。阅读偏好、主题设置及阅读进度仅通过 chrome.storage 存储在您的设备本地。

No Tracking / 不进行追踪

The extension does not track browsing history, IP addresses, or online activity.

本扩展不会追踪您的浏览历史、IP 地址或在线活动。

3. Permissions Usage / 权限使用说明

  • storage – Saves user preferences locally. （用于在本地保存用户偏好设置。）
  • scripting & activeTab – Applies reader mode to the currently active page after user activation. （在用户主动启用后，对当前活动页面应用阅读模式。）
  • tabs – Supports navigation between the original page and reader view. （用于在原始页面与阅读界面之间进行导航支持。）
  • declarativeNetRequest – Applies layout filtering rules required for reader mode rendering. （用于应用阅读模式所需的页面布局过滤规则。）
  • host permissions (<all_urls>) – Required to support chapter navigation through background fetch within the active tab’s origin. （为支持在活动标签页源站点范围内通过后台请求实现章节导航而必需。）

4. Data Sharing / 数据共享

No user data is sold, shared, or transmitted to third parties.

不会向任何第三方出售、共享或传输用户数据。

5. Changes to This Policy / 政策变更

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page.

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何更新都将在本页面发布。
