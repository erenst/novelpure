FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions / 常见问题

Why does Chrome show a "caution" or "data access" warning?

为什么 Chrome 会显示“安全警告”或“数据访问”提示？

1. Caution: As a new developer, it takes time to build trust with Google's Safe Browsing system.
2. Data Access: To reformat chapter content into a consistent reading layout， the extension must access the page content you are reading. NovelPure does not collect, store, or transmit your data.

1. 安全警告： 作为新开发者，建立 Google 安全浏览信任需要时间。
2. 权限说明： 为了将章节内容重新排版为统一的阅读结构，插件必须读取当前页面内容。 NovelPure 不会 收集、存储或上传任何用户数据。

Does NovelPure collect my data?

No. All processing happens locally. We have no remote servers.

不会。所有处理均在本地完成，我们没有远程服务器。

Is NovelPure safe to use?

Yes. The extension runs entirely inside your browser.

是的。插件完全在浏览器内部运行。

Does it modify the original website?

No. It only re-renders content locally and does not interfere with the website.

不会。仅在本地重塑页面显示，不会修改原网站。

How does NovelPure make money?

The extension is free. We may earn a small commission via affiliate links on this website (not inside the extension).

扩展完全免费。我们可能通过官网的联盟链接获得少量收入。
